Returns and Exchanges


According to the Law of Ukraine "On Protection of Consumer Rights" the buyer has the right to exchange the product for a similar one within 14 days, if the purchased product does not fit in terms of shape, dimensions, style, color, size or for other reasons, and if the product appearance, labels and settlement document issued by the seller together with the product are preserved.
