    Let's get to know each other, my name is Yakov.

    ﻿I am the founder of the FUN GRILL brand.

    I am the person who all holidays, trips to nature, picnics and family events are held separately, near the barbecue. I definitely enjoy cooking goodies for family and friends, but I often thought about how to make the process of preparing to culinary heroism - simpler, clearer and less costly in terms of time, attention and spent energy. Well, you know what I'm talking about? I am about all these rituals: gymnastic exercises aimed at fanning the fire, watering with smelly liquid, long time for waiting of coal. All the most interesting, all that is remembered in every holiday and events at this time passes by. Look, tthe children have grown up, until you're frozen and sweaty, going back in the circle families from the hearth. Does this sound familiar to you?

    This is how the idea arose to create a unique, simple, very convenient and available for use - charcoal set FUN GRILL.

    We created not just a product, it is a philosophy, understood and, I am sure, close to many. The time we spend with loved ones, it is necessary to be as productive as possible and with pleasure to spend on recreation and communication. Together i, my friends, and like-minded people, we created a company with a goal - to create comfort with the help of our product, convenience, simplicity, environmental friendliness, and most importantly - to save you money valuable time and energy needed for much more important things.

    Our values and philosophy are shared not only in Ukraine, but also in countries of the European Union. Today, FUN GRILL was well appreciated in Germany, our product has attracted the attention of many buyers in of Italy We are very happy that our view of recreation is winning more and more supporters among lovers of grill cooking. We invite you to evaluate all the advantages and convenience with charcoal set FUN GRILL.
